$$
\begin{align}
&\text{1. } \sin \alpha \sin \beta = \frac{\cos (\alpha-\beta)-\cos(\alpha+\beta)}{2} \\
&\text{2. } \cos \alpha \sin \beta = \frac{\sin (\alpha+\beta)-\sin (\alpha-\beta)}{2}
\end{align}
$$
